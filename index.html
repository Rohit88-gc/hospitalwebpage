Our Specialities

Neurology

Neurology focuses on diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, which includes the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Common neurological conditions include stroke, multiple sclerosis, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, and Alzheimer's disease. Treatments in neurology can range from medication management to complex neurosurgical procedures, aimed at restoring function and improving the quality of life for patients affected by neurological disorders.

Cardiology

Cardiology is a branch of medicine that deals with the disorders of the heart as well as some parts of the circulatory system. The field includes medical diagnosis and treatment of congenital heart defects, coronary artery disease, heart failure, valvular heart disease, and electrophysiology. Cardiologists ensure the heart health of patients and guide them through prevention and management of heart-related conditions.

Dentist

Dentistry is focused on the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases and conditions of the oral cavity and surrounding structures. It also includes the maintenance of the health of the teeth, gums, and other oral tissues. Services range from routine cleanings and fillings to more complex procedures such as root canals, crowns, and orthodontics.
